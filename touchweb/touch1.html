It seems that the obvious thing to do now, is to practice the respectful art of keeping distance.
So I wanted to create the paintings of sequence 4 by reducing the physical act and establish contact only once between the acomix and the canvas.
Before starting on the fundamentalistic approach, I did a purifying swipe-painting, as seen in the background. Then I hit the first canvas, focusing on the infection I had from the word ‘touch’.
